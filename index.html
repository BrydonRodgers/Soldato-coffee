The Mission

Our Story

Soldato was born from a single belief: that truly exceptional coffee is an act of discipline, not luck. We don't chase trends. We study the craft — sourcing, roasting, and delivering with the precision of a soldier who never cuts corners.

Our beans are selected from single-origin farms where the elevation, soil, and harvest season are tracked with the same rigour a general brings to a campaign. Every batch is small. Every roast is monitored to the second. Nothing ships unless it meets the standard.

We are proudly South African — rooted in the Western Cape and built on the belief that our country deserves world-class coffee on its own terms. No shortcuts. No compromises. Only the very best, every time.

Marco de Villiers
Founder & Head Roaster
